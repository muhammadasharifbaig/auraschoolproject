WHAT IS AN AI ?

AI, or Artificial Intelligence, is smart computer software that can learn and think like humans. It helps us do things faster and better by recognizing patterns and making decisions.

HOW AI HELPS CREATORS AND EDITORS ?

AI helps YouTube creators by suggesting titles, creating thumbnails, and adding subtitles. For video editors, AI can cut unwanted parts, improve video quality, and add effects, making the process faster and the videos look professional.

HOW AI HELPS STUDENTS IN CODING

AI assists coding students by explaining concepts, giving instant feedback, and suggesting improvements, making learning to code easier and more effective.
